bubble
tea

aka "boba"

Bubble tea or boba is a Taiwanese drink featuring a tea base with milk, tapioca pearls, and different toppings, making it an interactive drink and a sweet snack! The classic bubble tea is creamy and sweet with the essence of tea. The pearls are chewy with a tiny bit of mild sweetness. Bubble tea is a comforting and refreshing drink in any kind of setting.
